Beef Stroganoff

Discription

Named after a member of the Stroganoff family the dish has roots in Russian cuisine. In the US it mainly made of strips of beef filet with a mushroom, onion, and sour cream sauce, and is served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 ounces butter
  • 4 green onions, sliced (white parts only)
  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
